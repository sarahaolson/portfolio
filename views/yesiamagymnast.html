ABOUT

I have been making and posting videos on YouTube since I was 11 years old! When I was in high school, I noticed that the teams that I was on (Girls’ Varsity Swim and Dive and Varsity Gymnastics) did not receive the same level of recognition for their success compared to other more popular teams like Varsity Football or Hockey.

To combat this problem, I decided I would film at our meets and upload highlight reels to my YouTube channel to spread the word about our successes and create hype around our next competition. The links to my videos started getting reposted by other students, coaches, and even Framingham High School’s Athletics Department. I started being contacted by captains from other teams asking if their team could be featured in their own video. By the time COVID hit, I had made over 30 videos for teams, including Varsity Girls’ Swim and Dive, Varsity Gymnastics, Varsity Girls’ Volleyball, and JV Girls’ Lacrosse. I also collaborated with another student media group called MassTimes to create media for Varsity Football and Varsity Boys’ Soccer.
