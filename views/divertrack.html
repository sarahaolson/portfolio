Tools Used

An all in one platform that revolutionizes managing high school springboard diving teams by creating easy access to meet requirements, previous scores, and recruitment videos

MOTIVATION

I was a Varsity Diver during my first two years of High School! In my second season, I had a real chance of qualifying for MIAA Sectional and State Championships. Though I quickly realized, how confusing keeping track of my progress was as there are a lot of overlapping rules! So, I made an application to make my progress more visible.

The first iteration of this project was written in C++, then it was converted to Java during my High School years. Now DiverTrak stands as a complete web app with more capabilities, especially for coaches!
